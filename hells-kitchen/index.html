Hell's Kitchen

Running a kitchen can be pretty tough, even tougher when you're in a restaurant owned by Satan. It's worth it though, he's given you a chance to escape Hell if you do well enough. Will you make it back to Earth or even into heaven, or will you be stuck in Hell for all eternity?

This game was made for a UCF GameDev Knights GameJam
